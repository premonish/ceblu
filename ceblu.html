About

CEBLU is all about Cebu, Philippines!
Cebu has so much to offer to so many people.

Cebu, Philippines is a major hub for tourism in Southeast Asia. Cebu has so much depth for the adventurous and for the curious. We created this space for people to be able to share Cebu experiences. We are always looking for others to contribute with guest blogs, videos, or in any way you so desire. We are very happy to work with others who have something to share about Cebu! Simply get in touch with us to get involved,
